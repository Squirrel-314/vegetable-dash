Help Center

This is an incomplete guide of Vegetable Dash. If any old or experienced players want to make changes or add new pages, email me here: thehillsidetownship@gmail.com

Planting

Just press the button that says grow, then use a rare and powerfull skill called patience, and wait until it says harvest.

Harvesting

A button shall apear out of thin air, and written on it shall be that wonderful word: Harvest [Vegetabele Name]. You want to press that button. By doing this, you shall gain 1 vegetable of that type and 1 seed. By harvesting there is a 5% chance of collecting a Market Reset, a 10% chance of collection 2 extra vegeatbles, and a 20% chance of collecting 5 extra seeds.

Plots

Plots are where vegetables are grown. Each plot grows a certiant type of vegetable. To unlock the next plot, hover ver the large yellow lock and click 'purchase plot'. This shall cost seeds

Seeds

In this wonderful utopia, seeds are the only currency, most carrots are purple, and the sky is always blue except the times when it is not. Most thing require seeds such as unlocking plots and exchanging in the Market. You can collect them by selling produce in the market or harvesting vegetables.

Market

The market is a wonderful and bustling place filled with plenty of vegetables. It can be accessed by hovering over the bar in the bottom left corner then clicking the button with a shop icon. It show how much seeds you have in the first box. The next box has a the amount of a certiant type of vegetable you have, and you can sell 5 of them for an amount of seeds or buy 5 for some seeds. You can reset the price values with Market Resets

Black Market

The black market is a wonderful and bustling place filled with plenty of illegal items. It located at an undisclosed location. For an amount of seeds you can purchase illegal items such as Market Resets. But watch out! There is a 2% chance that the police will catch you and make you shall pay a terrible fine.

Market Resets

Market resets can be found as the last item in the Market. By using one all of the values in the market are reset to what they began as, not the outrageous price they have become. There is a 1% chance of collecting one after each trade, and a 5% chance after each harvest. One is gained every time you unlock a plot.

Fertilizer

Fertilizer is a useful item that when used on a vegetable plot instantly completes the growing and adds an extra vegetable of that type. Fertilizer can be bought in the Black Market, or very soon earned from tasks.
