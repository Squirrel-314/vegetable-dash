Help Center

This is an incomplete guide of Vegetable Dash. If any old or experienced players want to make changes or add new pages, email me here: thehillsidetownship@gmail.com

Planting

Just press the button that says grow, then use a rare and powerful skill called patience, and wait until it says harvest.

Harvesting

A button shall appear out of thin air, and written on it shall be that wonderful word: Harvest [Vegetable Name]. You want to press that button. By doing this, you shall gain 1 vegetable of that type and 1 seed. By harvesting there is a 5% chance of collecting a Market Reset, a 10% chance of collection 2 extra vegetables, and a 20% chance of collecting 5 extra seeds.

Plots

Plots are where vegetables are grown. Each plot grows a certain type of vegetable. To unlock the next plot, hover over the large yellow lock and click 'purchase plot'. This shall cost seeds

Seeds

In this wonderful utopia, seeds are the only currency, most carrots are purple, and the sky is always blue except the times when it is not. Most thing require seeds such as unlocking plots and exchanging in the Market. You can collect them by selling produce in the market or harvesting vegetables.

Market

The market is a wonderful and bustling place filled with plenty of vegetables. It can be accessed by hovering over the bar in the bottom left corner then clicking the button with a shop icon. It show how much seeds you have in the first box. The next box has a the amount of a certain type of vegetable you have, and you can sell 5 of them for an amount of seeds or buy 5 for some seeds. You can reset the price values with Market Resets

Market Resets

Market resets can be found as the last item in the Market. By using one all of the values in the market are reset to what they began as, not the outrageous price they have become. There is a 1% chance of collecting one after each trade, and a 5% chance after each harvest. One is gained every time you unlock a plot.

Fertilizer

Fertilizer is a useful item that when used on a vegetable plot instantly completes the growing and adds an extra vegetable of that type. Fertilizer can be bought in the Black Market (coming soon), or earned from Tasks.

Tasks

Tasks are small chores that Farmer Jebediah, Grandpa Jenkins, Grandma Josephine, or special guests request that require you to do a task for them or pay some items. They give helpful rewards like Fertilizer and Market Resets.

Farmer Jebediah

Previous owner of the farm, you closest adviser. He gives many important Tasks.

Grandpa Jenkins

Guide on everything farming, bearer of the Bunny Slippers, and giver of Tasks.

Grandma Josephine

Your personal economic adviser, and giver of Tasks.

Bunny Slippers

Currently borne by Grandpa Jenkins, the Bunny Slippers are the main object of the Bunny Slippers Task List, a Task List being a collection of related tasks. It begins with Grandma Josephine telling you to sell the slippers, then Grandpa Jenkins reveals their true value, and you must go on a long task list meeting many people who all want you to do something for them, finally ending with the retrieval of the Bunny Slippers.
